OUR STORY

From an abandoned property to a benchmark farm

Nearly two decades ago, the dream of building something meaningful was born among the hills of Southern Minas Gerais.

In the municipality of Campos Gerais, we found an old dairy property, abandoned but full of potential. With dedication and a long-term vision, that land was transformed into Fazenda Vale da Serra.

Today we cultivate more than 200 productive hectares focused on specialty coffee and expanding fruit-growing projects.

FOUNDER

Tomás Massao Tanoue

A descendant of Japanese immigrants and born in Tupã, São Paulo, Thomás built a career as an airplane pilot before deciding to dedicate his life to agriculture.

Today he leads Fazenda Vale da Serra with discipline, precision and a long-term vision, combining tradition, innovation and environmental responsibility.

HOW WE WORK

Our Philosophy

Sustainability is one of the pillars of Fazenda Vale da Serra. We use natural resources responsibly, reusing coffee husks as fuel for dryers and as organic compost for fertilizing the coffee fields. We combine modern agronomic techniques with sound environmental practices.

Reuse

Coffee husks are reused as fuel for dryers and as organic compost for fertilization.

Modern Techniques

We align the best agronomic practices with respect for the natural cycle of the land.

Environmental Responsibility

Every decision is made with a focus on preserving the local ecosystem and reducing environmental impact.
